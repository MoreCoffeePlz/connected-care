Home Environment

Pet friendly & Smoke Free

Read study

Research suggests that animal companionship can support children’s well-being. We have a friendly dog and a gentle cat in our home. Interactions are always supervised and we teach gentle, respectful handling.

First Aid Certified

Trained in First Aid/CPR (Level C)

We keep emergency supplies on hand, maintain up-to-date contacts, and follow a simple plan: stay calm, care for the child, contact families, and call 911 when needed. We teach age-appropriate safety protocols that empower children to participate in their safety and the safety of others.
